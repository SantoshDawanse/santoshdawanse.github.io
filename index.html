My Journey

The journey began in 2016 with Android development and freelancing—building mobile apps, teaching Computer Science, and learning the fundamentals of Java, Laravel, and MySQL. By 2019, I stepped into a leadership role as Mobile App Lead at Aayam Technologies, designing apps that communicated with IoT machines and building mortgage platforms with React and Firebase. These early years taught me to be resourceful and laid the foundation for solving real-world problems through software.

By 2021, I joined Truenary Solutions, where I discovered the power of modern infrastructure and cloud-native development. I migrated a React/Redux eCommerce platform to Next.js, implemented payment wallets, and provisioned AWS resources using AWS CDK. As a Senior Software Engineer, I deployed custom GitLab runners on AWS Fargate, scripted OTA updates, and built DevOps pipelines with GitLab CI. Working with TypeScript, Python, and Golang, I realized that great software isn't just about code—it's about scalable architecture and robust infrastructure.

The next chapter took me to fintech. At Rara Labs in 2022, I worked on core banking systems, diving deep into Golang and GraphQL. I wrote complex queries, built accounting systems, and learned how to handle money at scale. The precision required in fintech shaped how I think about reliability and data integrity.

Then came the pivot that changed everything. In 2023, I joined WhiteHat Engineering as a Senior Software Engineer and Team Lead. This is where I discovered my passion for AI and blockchain. I led a team building platforms that accelerate drug development—work that actually saves lives. We built agentic input systems for data extraction, created GraphRAG chatbots with actions, and implemented MOE agents. This wasn't just about AI—it was about using AI to solve problems that matter.

Today, as an AI Product Builder at Sprout AI Studio, I'm part of a venture studio building Relational AI—technology designed to enhance genuine human connection, not simulate it. I partner with visionary founders to turn AI ideas into market-ready ventures, working with LLMs, RAG systems, and AI agents using Python. We're building AI that amplifies human flourishing, taking ventures from ideation to launch. The journey from building simple web apps to architecting AI-powered solutions has taught me that technology is just a tool—what matters is the impact you create with it.

9 years across fintech, e-commerce, IoT, pharma tech, and education. From Android apps to AI systems. Every step building toward creating intelligent solutions that make a difference.
